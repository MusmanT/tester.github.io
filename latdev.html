Belay Device
Mountaineering Accessories

A belay device acts as a brake on the climbing rope by applying friction to it. It increases the friction rate of the rope helps in reducing the amount of total force which the belayer has to hold by the strength of his/hers arms. The device, plus the belayer's quick (which locks off the free end of the rope), helps keep tension on the rope and helps protect the climber at the other end. It's an essential device for climbing safety.
